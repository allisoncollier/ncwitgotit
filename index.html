What is Got IT?

Got IT is a two Saturday workshop for girls currently enrolled in 6th, 7th or 8th grade who are interested in learning about the fundamentals of computer science.

Program Details

When: February 14, 2015 | March 21, 2015

Time: 9am - 4pm

Where: Virginia Tech Blacksburg Campus

Cost: $20 (due at the first session on February 14, PERSONAL CHECKS ONLY). If you cannot pay the fee, you should still apply! We have scholarships to cover your cost!

Curriculum: MIT Scratch and CS Unplugged

Benefits of Attending


A Computer Game

By the end of the program, you will have a working computer game that you and your friends can play. Best of all, it will be created by YOU!

Professional Development

During the lunch break (lunch is provided during every session of Got IT), speakers from the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech and company representatives from the Computer Science Reserach Consortium will speak on various aspects of pursuing a degree and career in a technical field.

CS Basics

Got IT will cover basic concepts within computer science. By the end of the program, you will be prepared to go forth and conquer more complex topics.

About NCWIT: The National Center for Women and Information Technology (NCWIT) is a non-profit community of more than 575 universities, companies, non-profits, and government organizations nationwide working to increase women’s participation in computing and technology.
